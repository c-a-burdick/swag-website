Tools for Your Journey

SWAG, LLC provides a comprehensive reentry framework designed to address the most critical barriers individuals face following incarceration. Each program pillar is intentionally structured to promote stability, growth, responsibility, and long-term independence.

A Foundation for New Beginnings

Transitional Housing Program

Our housing program is far more than shelter—it is a sanctuary for renewal and a launching point for transformation.
Men returning from incarceration often face overwhelming obstacles. Through safe and structured transitional housing, SWAG, LLC creates an environment where stability replaces uncertainty and hope replaces despair.
Within this environment, residents are surrounded by accountability, mentorship, and resources that allow them to rebuild their lives with dignity and confidence.

Key Elements
  • Safe, federally sanctioned housing
  • A structured living environment rooted in accountability, discipline, and respect
  • Access to critical life resources that restore confidence and stability
Vision

To provide every participant with a foundation strong enough to support a future filled with purpose, opportunity, and possibility.

Changing Your Narrative
Cognitive Behavioral Training (UCCI CORE Curriculum)

True transformation begins with a renewed mindset.
Through evidence-based cognitive behavioral programming, participants learn to challenge destructive thinking patterns and replace them with constructive decision making and emotional discipline.
This program directly addresses the psychological barriers and trauma that often accompany incarceration while equipping individuals with the tools needed to move forward with clarity and purpose.
Participants begin to reshape the narrative of their lives—shifting from survival to growth, from regret to responsibility, and from limitation to opportunity.

Key Elements
  • Transformational group-based learning sessions
  • Personalized one-on-one coaching and mentorship
  • Tools for emotional regulation, problem-solving, and responsible decision-making
Vision

To awaken the inner strength, clarity, and resilience necessary for individuals to move forward with intention, self-awareness, and confidence.

Structured Growth Through Achievement
Residential Merit Advancement Program

Our housing model is intentionally designed to reinforce discipline, accountability, and measurable personal growth.
Advancement within the housing program is earned through demonstrated responsibility, participation in programming, and consistent behavioral progress.
This merit-based structure encourages residents to pursue excellence while recognizing their achievements.

Progression Structure
Level One: Foundational Placement
  • Two-bedroom shared unit
  • No television
  • Emphasis on discipline, structure, and personal accountability
Level Two: Achievement Advancement
  • Two-bedroom shared unit
  • Television included
  • Earned through consistent program participation and positive accomplishment evaluations
Level Three: Premier Advancement
  • One-bedroom unit with television
  • Bathroom-adjacent layout
  • Reserved for residents demonstrating leadership, discipline, and program excellence

This progressive system reinforces a powerful truth: growth produces tangible rewards. It promotes delayed gratification, internal motivation, and accountability—key components of lasting reintegration success.

Vision

To cultivate a residential environment where progress is visible, earned, and directly tied to personal development—encouraging every resident to strive for excellence and sustained transformation.
