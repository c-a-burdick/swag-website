What We Do

A Structured, Holistic Path Back to the Workforce

Our mission is to equip formerly incarcerated men with the structure, skills, and unwavering support necessary for successful and sustainable reentry into society. Through transitional housing, UCCI-based cognitive behavioral programming, personal accountability, and opportunity-driven programming, we provide a pathway toward transformation.

At SWAG, LLC, we believe that every man deserves the opportunity to rise above his past, reclaim his future, and become the strongest version of himself. Our purpose is not simply to assist individuals after incarceration—but to inspire lasting change, restore confidence, and build productive members of our communities.



OUR EIGHT-PILLAR PROGRAM MODEL

🏠

Transitional Housing

Our housing program is far more than shelter—it is a sanctuary for renewal and a launching point for transformation.

⛓️‍💥

Cognitive Behavioral Training

Through evidence-based cognitive behavioral programming, participants learn to challenge destructive thinking patterns and replace them with constructive decision making and emotional discipline.

💼

Employment Through Empowerment

Our Employment Empowerment Program prepares participants to enter the workforce equipped with the skills, professionalism, and discipline necessary for long-term success.

🚗

Transportation Support

SWAG, LLC removes this obstacle by ensuring participants have consistent and dependable access to employment, educational programs, and required appointments.

📝

Career and Trade Resource Navigator

Our Career and Trade Resource Navigators help residents identify their strengths and translate them into sustainable career paths.

🏡

Long-Term Housing Assistance

SWAG, LLC assists residents in transitioning from transitional housing into sustainable living environments where stability and independence can thrive.

👨‍🍳

Nutritional Support Program

SWAG, LLC ensures that each resident receives consistent and nourishing meals that support health, focus, and daily performance.

📺

Residential Merit Advancement

Advancement within the housing program is earned through demonstrated responsibility, participation in programming, and consistent behavioral progress.
