Support SWAG

How you can help

While we work on getting our program up and running, there are a number of things you can to do help us make a difference in the lives of our future clients.

Spread the Word

Connect with your community

One of the simplest ways you can help is to tell people about SWAG, LLC and share this website with them. It will continue to be updated as the program moves toward its active phase.


Connect with your network

Do you have professional connections with other local non-profits working to make a difference in the lives of systemically disadvantaged young men? Help us develop partnerships that will make our program more robust and resilient. Drop us a line at swagcincinnati@gmail.com!


Get involved

Donate your time

Do you have experience developing grant proposals? Maybe you're a skilled organizational leader. Or maybe you just want to help set up tables at a community event. If you want to help us make progress towards our goals by lending a hand, please let us know at swagcincinnati@gmail.com.


Donate money

Coming Soon

We are actively pursuing grants to fund our program, but we will appreciate any financial support you can provide. (At this time, we are still working on setting up our online donation portal.)
